Mentors

A crucial part of our platform is the role of the Mentors. Mentors will predominantly be student volunteers from grade 11 and 12, well acquainted with Python. A set of 5 students will each be assigned a Mentor, who will help guide them through their journey of learning Python, and will also act as a confidant and a friend. Mentors will also give direction and viable outlets to eavh student, to help them pursue whichever Computer Science field they wish to. The Mentor-Mentee bond would transcend simple coding doubts to one of true understanding and friendship.

Community

One of our most crucial aims is to build a sisterhood of sorts, comprising all our students seeking to learn how to code and Mentors. This community would expose our students to a like-minded group of their peers which will help inspire and motivate them. Our sisterhood will be a tight knit community and, would act as a safe haven for girls to share absolutely anything they wish to-reiterating a sense of belonging and positivity.
