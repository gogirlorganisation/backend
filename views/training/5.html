Roman Numerals

Eve has a math test tomorrow, the topic being tested is Roman Numerals. She has trouble understanding it, thus has to look at multiple examples. Help her by making a program that converts numbers into Roman Numerals. Keep in mind some of the rules listed below:

  1. Use the following symbols:

    Roman Numeral Numeric Form
    I 1
    V 5
    X 10
    L 50
    C 100
    D 500
    M 1000


  2. Only I,X,M and C can be repeated thrice.

  3. Only the following subtractions can occur:

    Roman Numeral Numeric Form
    IV 4
    IX 9
    XL 40
    XC 90
    CD 400
    CM 900
